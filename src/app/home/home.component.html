Who we are and what we do.

OpenConnect, based in Dallas, is a leader in operational intelligence and automation. Our solutions enable you to solve these needs:

  • The need to know what your workforce really does.
  • The need to optimize your workforce’s productivity.
  • The need to address, and adapt to, today’s business challenges.
  • The need to access all your data.

Our products include:

  • WorkiQ — A real-time desktop analytics solution that provides visibility into employees’ work and productivity.
  • AutoiQ — Designed to automate routine tasks and workflows while improving first-pass efficiency.
  • Connect — Mainframe integration, the right way.
  • WebConnect — Client-/server-based software (now available as an HTML 5 client) that enables enterprise organizations to provide suppliers, partners, and employees with secure access to vital applications and information.

WebConnect

With its patented, secure, “persistent connectivity” technology, only OpenConnect’s WebConnect™ is capable of supporting tens of thousands of concurrent browser-based users. WebConnect is client-/server-based software that enables enterprise organizations to provide suppliers, partners, and employees with secure access to vital applications and information. With WebConnect, enterprises increase productivity and profits, and retain all the advantages of secure host connectivity to new and existing applications in real time.

WebConnect can be configured to provide secure communication to your host environment across a variety of underlying client technologies.
